About orchardium

Orchardium is the brand-new technology for farming and planting that offers every farmer a chance to monitor his fields by giving him all data associated with soil and atmosphere affecting the plant.

All perspective users will own a robot that has the ability to measure soil moisture, temperature, light intensity and humidity. These readings are essential for the farmer to know how far weather and soil conditions are affecting his plants. The robot is provided with camera to take pictures and, in the future, detects diseases and growth rate of the plant. 

The user will have the opportunity to monitor all data the robot measures using our mobile application. Every user will have an account on our database, where the user's data is stored then rerouted to him as numerical and graphical values. Our mobile application is provided by a notification system that notifies the user with success milestones, or alerts him in cases of danger.

It is amazing to see agriculture improves using modern technology where robots take care of the "handy" work and the farmer only has to keep an eye on functioning the whole system. The integration between software and hardware opens millions of doors for future ideas and modifications. Orchardium aims to progress further more and develop other versions of the current technology. Orchardium has a near-future plan to make a twin for V1.0; another robot that is stationary for a specific area of plants(all connected to its own software).
